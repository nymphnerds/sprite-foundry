# Subject Sheet — Thal

## Role
Ship engineer aboard a merchant freighter. Keth Communion native — arthropod biology, organic engineering tradition. Builds by growing, repairs by bonding. Mid-career, exile from Communion Relay.

## Consumer
Star Freight (sci-fi space merchant RPG)

## Generation Target
48x48 pixel art sprite, 8 directions, HD-2D style

---

## Silhouette

Non-human. The silhouette must read "insectoid engineer" not "human in a bug costume." Thal is bipedal but proportioned differently: slightly hunched forward posture, longer arms relative to torso, narrower waist, broader thorax (upper back/shoulder region). The chitin plating on forearms and shins creates angular bulk at the extremities.

**Silhouette priority (what must read at 48px):**
1. Hunched posture — forward lean, head slightly below shoulder line
2. Chitin forearm guards — angular bulk on both forearms
3. Antennae — two short swept-back antennae from crown of head
4. Thorax width — upper back/shoulders broader than waist
5. Tool harness across chest — diagonal strap with organic pouches

---

## Physical Description

- **Species:** Keth — arthropod-adjacent, bipedal, exoskeletal highlights over softer underlayer
- **Build:** Lean but angular. Thorax (upper back) is the widest point. Waist narrows. Arms slightly long for body.
- **Height proportion:** Slightly shorter than human average, but wider at shoulders
- **Posture:** Forward hunch, head carried ahead of center of mass. Working posture, not servile.
- **Skin/surface:** Matte olive-brown base skin with darker chitin plates on forearms, shins, and shoulder ridge. Not glossy — weathered chitin, like old bark.
- **Head:** Slightly elongated, no hair. Two compound-style eyes (larger than human, dark amber). Small mandible structures at jaw (subtle, not monstrous). Two antennae swept back from crown — short, functional, not dramatic.
- **Hands:** Four-fingered, slightly longer than human. Chitin-backed.

## Must-Stay-True (non-negotiable across all 8 directions)

1. Forward hunch posture — head NEVER upright above shoulders
2. Two antennae visible from front, side, and 3/4 views
3. Chitin forearm guards on BOTH arms — angular, not smooth
4. Tool harness diagonal from LEFT shoulder to RIGHT hip
5. Non-human proportions — thorax wider than waist, arms slightly long
6. Four-fingered hands (when hands are visible)
7. Matte surface — no shine, no gloss, no metal highlights

## Forbidden Drift

- Human-proportioned body (normal waist-to-shoulder ratio)
- Glossy/wet-looking chitin (Thal is weathered, not fresh)
- Insect wings of any kind
- More than two antennae
- Mandibles that dominate the face (subtle jaw structures only)
- Any weapon or military gear
- Bright colors or neon bioluminescence (the communion mark is pale, not glowing)
- Upright military posture
