# Subject Sheet — Hull Diver

## Role
Compact-origin EVA repair specialist. Works outside the hull in vacuum — welding, patching, structural inspection. Working-class crew, not military. Same civilization as the Compact Patrol Officer but completely different silhouette — this is the blue-collar counterpart.

## Consumer
Star Freight (sci-fi space merchant RPG)

## Generation Target
48x48 pixel art sprite, 8 directions, HD-2D style

---

## Silhouette

Bulky and industrial. The helmet and backpack are the two must-hold shape anchors — they are the entire point of this subject as a silhouette pressure test. Everything reads as "heavy sealed work gear."

**Silhouette priority (what must read at 48px):**
1. Dome helmet with narrow visor slit — the top shape anchor
2. Welding pack on back — the rear shape anchor, must survive rotation
3. Thick gauntlets — oversized hands
4. Magnetic boot clamps — heavy feet
5. Yellow-orange hazard stripes — the color identifier

---

## Physical Description

- **Species:** Human (Terran Compact origin)
- **Build:** Stocky, muscular. The suit adds bulk on top of that.
- **Posture:** Slightly forward-leaning from backpack weight. Working stance, not military attention.
- **Face:** Not visible — sealed dome helmet with narrow horizontal visor slit
- **Suit surface:** Matte, scuffed, scratched from void work. Not clean. Not new.

## Must-Stay-True (non-negotiable across all 8 directions)

1. Dome helmet always visible — the top silhouette anchor
2. Welding pack on back — visible from side, back, and 3/4 rear views
3. Hazard stripes — must read as the color identifier in all directions
4. Heavy boots — feet always chunky
5. Thick gauntlets — hands always oversized
6. Matte scuffed surface — NOT clean, NOT sleek

## Forbidden Drift

- Exposed face or hair (fully sealed helmet)
- Sleek sci-fi armor (this is industrial work gear)
- Military tactical appearance
- Iron Man / power armor aesthetic
- Clean/pristine suit surface
- Bright vivid colors (only the hazard stripes have color)
- Weapons of any kind
- Thin/slim build visible through suit
- Cape, wings, or flowing elements
- Astronaut floating / zero gravity pose (standing on ground)
