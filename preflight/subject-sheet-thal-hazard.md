# Subject Sheet — Thal (Hazard Suit)

## Role
Ship engineer aboard a merchant freighter, wearing sealed hazard protection gear for hull breach / toxic atmosphere duty. Same individual as base Thal — Keth Communion native, arthropod biology. This is an outfit variant, not a new character.

## Consumer
Star Freight (sci-fi space merchant RPG)

## Generation Target
48x48 pixel art sprite, 8 directions, HD-2D style

## Variant Contract
This is an outfit variant of the proven subject **Thal**. The following identity anchors MUST survive the costume change:

1. Forward hunch posture — same as base Thal
2. Two short swept-back antennae — protruding through helmet seal gaskets
3. Compound amber eyes — visible through open-face visor
4. Chitin forearm plates — visible through transparent suit sleeve panels
5. Four-fingered hands — in sealed gloves
6. Non-human proportions — thorax wider than waist, arms slightly long
7. Mandible jaw structures — visible, not hidden by mask

---

## Silhouette

Same alien body as base Thal, but bulkier from hazard suit. Key silhouette changes:

**What changes from base Thal:**
- Tool harness → sealed utility belt
- Work apron → full hazard suit torso
- Exposed chitin → suit covers most skin
- Organic patch kit → replaced by suit equipment

**What must NOT change:**
- Hunched posture
- Antennae presence and angle
- Compound eye visibility
- Overall alien proportions
- Forearm chitin still readable through suit panels

**Silhouette priority (what must read at 48px):**
1. Hunched posture with hazard suit bulk
2. Antennae protruding through helmet seal
3. Heavier torso — sealed suit reads thicker than base Thal
4. Sealed collar / open-face visor around head
5. Utility belt at waist

## Must-Stay-True (non-negotiable across all 8 directions)

1. Forward hunch posture — head NEVER upright above shoulders
2. Two antennae visible from front, side, and 3/4 views — through helmet seal
3. Compound amber eyes visible — visor is open-face, not sealed
4. Chitin forearm plates readable through suit panels on BOTH arms
5. Non-human proportions — thorax wider than waist, arms slightly long
6. Matte surface — suit is industrial, not sleek sci-fi

## Forbidden Drift

- Fully sealed helmet hiding face (visor must be open-face)
- Antennae hidden or missing
- Human proportions inside the suit
- Glossy/sleek sci-fi armor (this is work gear, not combat suit)
- Base Thal's tool harness appearing (replaced by utility belt)
- Base Thal's work apron appearing (replaced by full suit)
- Military/tactical appearance
- Bright colors or neon elements
