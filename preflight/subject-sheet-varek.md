# Subject Sheet — Varek

## Role
Ship gunner aboard a merchant freighter. Veshan Principalities exile — reptilian warrior caste, House Drashan disgraced. Trained fighter carrying honor debt. Late 20s equivalent, heavy build.

## Consumer
Star Freight (sci-fi space merchant RPG)

## Generation Target
48x48 pixel art sprite, 8 directions, HD-2D style

---

## Silhouette

The biggest silhouette in the crew. Varek must read "armored gunner" not "knight" or "soldier." The shoulder pauldron on the LEFT is the dominant mass. The sidearm holster on RIGHT thigh is the secondary anchor. Overall posture is upright and squared — military bearing that contrasts with Thal's hunch and Sera's practical lean.

**Silhouette priority (what must read at 48px):**
1. LEFT shoulder pauldron — single large armored plate, asymmetric bulk
2. Broad chest in fitted combat vest
3. Sidearm holster on RIGHT thigh
4. Scaled skin texture on exposed arms
5. Jaw ridge / head crest — reptilian profile reads even small

---

## Physical Description

- **Species:** Veshan — reptilian-adjacent, bipedal, scaled skin, internal skeleton with external ridging
- **Build:** Heavy, broad-shouldered, barrel-chested. Tallest crew member. Arms thick with muscle.
- **Height proportion:** Taller and wider than human average
- **Posture:** Upright, squared shoulders. Military bearing. Weight evenly distributed.
- **Skin/surface:** Muted grey-green scales, slightly rougher/raised along jawline and forearms. Not bright green — desaturated, like old stone. Scales are small and tight, not individual plates.
- **Head:** Broader than human, slightly flattened. Low brow ridge with subtle crest running center-back (not a mohawk — a natural ridge, like a crocodilian). Eyes narrow, amber-gold with vertical pupils. Strong jaw with scaled ridge extending to ear-line. No external ears — smooth scaled sides.
- **Hands:** Five-fingered but slightly broader, with visible scale texture. Short claws (not dramatic).

## Must-Stay-True (non-negotiable across all 8 directions)

1. LEFT shoulder pauldron — always the bulkiest point of the silhouette from any angle
2. Reptilian head profile — brow ridge, jaw ridge, head crest visible in profile and 3/4
3. Scaled skin on exposed arms — texture must read, not smooth
4. Sidearm holster on RIGHT thigh — drop-leg position, not hip
5. Upright military posture — squared shoulders, weight centered
6. No bright colors — everything is worn, desaturated, functional
7. Broader and taller than other crew silhouettes

## Forbidden Drift

- Bright green or vivid reptilian colors (Varek is grey-green, desaturated)
- Tail of any kind (Veshan are bipedal, no tails in this design)
- Full plate armor or medieval knight aesthetic
- Two shoulder pauldrons (LEFT only — the asymmetry is the point)
- Sword or melee weapon as primary (Varek is a gunner — sidearm, not blade)
- Smiling or friendly expression (neutral/stern military bearing)
- Hunched or crouching posture (upright always)
- Horns, wings, or fantasy dragon features
- Smooth/human skin texture on exposed areas
