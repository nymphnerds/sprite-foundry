# Subject Sheet — Veshan House Envoy

## Role
Diplomatic officer representing a Veshan noble house. Political authority figure — not a fighter. Power reads through tailoring, posture, and bearing, not through weapons or armor. The Veshan are the honor/hierarchy civilization in Star Freight.

## Consumer
Star Freight (sci-fi space merchant RPG)

## Generation Target
48x48 pixel art sprite, 8 directions, HD-2D style

---

## Silhouette

Tall, formal, upright. The long structured coat is the primary silhouette element — it must read as "authority and wealth" not "military" or "tactical." No visible weapon. No armor. The power comes from the cut of the coat and the straightness of the posture.

**Silhouette priority (what must read at 48px):**
1. Long structured coat reaching below knees — the dominant shape
2. High stiff collar — reads as formal authority
3. Perfect upright posture — contrast against all other characters
4. Gold trim at front closure and cuffs — minimal but visible
5. Thin gold chain across chest between collar clasps

---

## Physical Description

- **Species:** Veshan — humanoid alien, pale blue-grey skin, angular features
- **Build:** Tall, lean. No bulk. Elegance, not mass.
- **Posture:** Perfectly upright, almost rigid. Chin level. Shoulders back. No slouch, no combat-ready lean.
- **Skin:** Pale blue-grey, smooth
- **Face:** High cheekbones, sharp angular jaw, narrow features. Cold amber eyes. Faint geometric tattoo markings on temples and jaw line.
- **Hair:** Silver-white, swept straight back, short and controlled
- **Hands:** At sides or one hand resting on coat front — diplomatic, not aggressive

## Must-Stay-True (non-negotiable across all 8 directions)

1. Long coat below the knees — NEVER shorter
2. High stiff collar — must read in every direction
3. Perfect upright posture — no lean, no hunch, no combat stance
4. No visible weapon — ZERO weapons anywhere
5. Pale blue-grey alien skin tone
6. Gold trim reads as formal, not flashy

## Forbidden Drift

- ANY visible weapon (gun, blade, holster, sheath)
- Body armor, tactical vest, patrol gear
- Military uniform appearance (this is diplomatic, not military)
- Patrol officer silhouette leakage (no badge, no insignia bar, no comm unit)
- Casual or ragged clothing
- Bright saturated colors
- Cape or flowing robes (structured coat, not wizard)
- Combat-ready stance or aggressive posture
- Pirate / scavenger elements
