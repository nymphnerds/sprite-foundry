# Subject Sheet — Ilen Marr

## Role
Logistics tech and convoy scheduling specialist aboard a merchant freighter. Orryn Drift native — mobile broker culture, neutral by policy, information as currency. Early 30s, wiry build, always reading data.

## Consumer
Star Freight (sci-fi space merchant RPG)

## Generation Target
48x48 pixel art sprite, 8 directions, HD-2D style

---

## Silhouette

The lightest silhouette in the crew — intentionally. Ilen's visual identity comes from posture and gear placement, not bulk. The key read is "data worker in motion" — slight forward lean (reading something), scanner unit on LEFT forearm, messenger bag slung RIGHT. The challenge is that Ilen has less dramatic mass than any other crew member, so landmark placement must be precise to survive 48px.

**Silhouette priority (what must read at 48px):**
1. Scanner unit on LEFT forearm — rectangular block, always visible
2. Messenger bag on RIGHT hip/thigh — angular, strap crosses chest
3. Slim build with slight forward lean
4. Short cropped hair with no dramatic features
5. High-collared jacket — defines the neckline distinctly

---

## Physical Description

- **Species:** Human-adjacent (Orryn Drift population is human-stock, culturally distinct)
- **Build:** Wiry, narrow-shouldered, lean. Not fragile — efficient. Moves like someone who grew up on stations with variable gravity.
- **Height proportion:** Average height, notably narrower than other crew
- **Posture:** Slight forward lean — habitual data-reading posture. Head tilted slightly down. Not a hunch (unlike Thal) — more like someone perpetually checking a screen.
- **Skin:** Warm brown, clear. No scars, no tattoos visible.
- **Hair:** Short-cropped black, tight to the skull. No styling. Practical.
- **Face:** Angular, high cheekbones. Alert expression — always scanning, assessing. Dark brown eyes.
- **Hands:** Slim, dexterous. LEFT hand often near the scanner unit.

## Must-Stay-True (non-negotiable across all 8 directions)

1. Forearm scanner on LEFT arm — always visible, rectangular shape
2. Messenger bag on RIGHT hip with diagonal strap
3. Slim/wiry build — noticeably narrower than Sera, much narrower than Varek
4. High collar on jacket — defines the neckline, visible from all angles
5. Slight forward lean — data-reading posture, not upright military
6. No weapons visible — Ilen is not a fighter
7. Cool neutral palette — no warm accents except skin tone

## Forbidden Drift

- Bulky or muscular build (Ilen is wiry, not athletic)
- Bright or saturated clothing (everything is muted, professional)
- Armor, weapons, or tactical gear of any kind
- Long hair, dramatic hairstyle, or headgear
- Lab coat, medical scrubs, or scientist aesthetic (Ilen is logistics, not science)
- Glowing holographic interfaces (the scanner is a physical device with a small dim screen)
- Backpack instead of messenger bag (the bag is angular and hip-mounted)
- Upright confident stance (Ilen leans forward slightly, perpetually reading)
- Goggles, visor, or face-covering gear
