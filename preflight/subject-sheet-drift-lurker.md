# Subject Sheet — Drift Lurker

## Role
Medium quadrupedal ambush predator from the Orryn Drift shipping lanes. Opportunistic hunter — lurks in asteroid debris and derelict hulls, ambushes small prey and scavengers. Not domesticated, not friendly. A natural hazard of the Drift.

## Consumer
Star Freight (sci-fi space merchant RPG)

## Generation Target
48x48 pixel art sprite, 8 directions, HD-2D style

## Morphology
**Body family:** Quadruped (proven lane — same as Cargo Beast)
**Stack:** A_v2_morph (ControlNet Depth)
**Key difference from Cargo Beast:** Leaner, lower, wider stance. Predator, not pack animal. Head forward, not head low-and-down.

---

## Silhouette

Low-slung horizontal body. Four thick legs in a wide splayed stance. Broad flat wedge-shaped head thrust forward. Armored dorsal ridge running the full spine. Long tapered whip tail. Everything about this shape says "ambush predator" — low, wide, ready.

**Must NOT collapse into Cargo Beast:**
- No pack animal mass (Lurker is lean, not barrel-shaped)
- No saddle or harness
- No upward head carriage (head is forward and low)
- No domesticated / gentle demeanor

**Silhouette priority (what must read at 48px):**
1. Low horizontal body — never upright, never tall
2. Wedge-shaped head thrust forward — predator head read
3. Armored dorsal ridge — overlapping plates along spine
4. Four wide-splayed legs — crouched ready stance
5. Long whip tail — extends from rear

---

## Physical Description

- **Body plan:** Low-slung quadruped, lizard/large cat hybrid proportions
- **Size:** Medium — roughly waist-height to a human, but longer than tall
- **Legs:** Four thick muscular legs with wide splayed clawed feet
- **Body:** Long horizontal, held low to the ground. Lean but powerful.
- **Dorsal ridge:** Overlapping dark armored plates from skull to tail base
- **Head:** Broad, flat, wedge-shaped. Wide jaw. Pale slit-pupil predatory eyes. Short powerful neck.
- **Tail:** Long, tapered, whip-like. Used for balance in ambush lunges.
- **Hide:** Mottled dark grey-green leathery skin. Faint bioluminescent blue-green spots along flanks (dim, not glowing).

## Must-Stay-True (non-negotiable across all 8 directions)

1. Low horizontal body — NEVER standing tall or upright
2. Head thrust FORWARD — predator ready position, not head-down grazing
3. Four legs in wide splayed stance — crouched, not standing tall
4. Dorsal ridge visible from every angle — the armored spine
5. Long whip tail from rear
6. NOT a pack animal — no saddle, no harness, no cargo

## Forbidden Drift

- Upright or vertical posture (this creature is horizontal)
- Pack animal / domesticated appearance (no saddle, no harness)
- Cargo Beast body mass (Lurker is lean, not barrel-shaped)
- Cute or friendly demeanor
- Bright vivid colors or neon bioluminescence (spots are DIM)
- Wings or flying elements
- Bipedal stance of any kind
- Insect / arthropod body plan (this is a quadruped)
- Robot or mechanical elements
- Six or more legs
