# Subject Sheet — Compact Interdiction Agent

## Role
Internal affairs / enforcement agent of the Terran Compact. Not military, not police — a bureaucratic threat. Investigates smuggling, regulatory violations, license fraud. The kind of person who ends your career with a data pad, not a gun. Hostile class, but the menace is institutional, not violent.

## Consumer
Star Freight (sci-fi space merchant RPG)

## Generation Target
48x48 pixel art sprite, 8 directions, HD-2D style

---

## Silhouette

Clean, fitted, dark. Closer to Nera Quill's institutional world than to the pirate/scav lane. The coat is tailored and dark — not a duster, not a uniform. The data gauntlet on the left wrist is the key identifier. The sidearm is concealed, barely visible. This character threatens through presence and authority, not through visible weapons.

**CRITICAL — must NOT collapse into:**
- Compact Patrol Officer (no visible armor, no comm unit, no rank bar)
- Reach Pirate (no duster, no trophy gorget, no faction tag)
- Scav Raider (no ragged gear, no balaclava, no welded weapons)

**Silhouette priority (what must read at 48px):**
1. Dark fitted long coat — mid-thigh, clean cut, minimal detail
2. Data gauntlet on LEFT wrist — the unique identifier
3. High turndown collar — not military stiff, not casual open
4. Upright precise posture — controlled, not rigid military
5. Matte black ID disc on RIGHT lapel — small but present

---

## Physical Description

- **Species:** Human (Terran Compact origin)
- **Build:** Lean, wiry. Not bulky. Precise.
- **Posture:** Upright but not at attention. Controlled stillness. Weight balanced.
- **Face:** Sharp narrow features, thin lips, cold grey-green eyes. Clean-shaven, short dark hair combed back.
- **Hands:** Thin dark gloves. Hands at sides — not reaching for weapon, not clenched.
